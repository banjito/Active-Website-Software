# Dev Log — May 21 to June 4, 2026

## Calendar and opportunity workflow

**Problem:** Proposal work was harder to scan from the calendar. Opportunity calendar highlights also focused on due-soon status, which did not always match what the team needed to act on.

**Fix:** Added a week-view calendar and made proposal calendar access available from shortcuts. Updated opportunity calendar highlights to show estimate stage instead of due-soon status, so the calendar better reflects sales progress.

**Why it matters:** The team can now see proposal timing and estimate stage faster, without opening extra screens.

## Login, profile, and account UX

**Problem:** Login and profile flows needed cleaner handling. Users also needed clearer personal account controls.
